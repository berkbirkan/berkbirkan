# 💫 About Me:
I am Software Engineer & AI Engineer from Turkey.

I have been coding since the age of 8. I started my programming journey with Visual Basic 6.0 and developed simple desktop applications. In middle school, I continued with PHP and built on my skills.

In high school and college, I expanded my knowledge of programming with Android and iOS development. Over the years, I have gained a great deal of experience and developed a passion for coding.

In 2023, I founded NEURALABZ LTD, a company developing SaaS products based on AI Agents and automations.

I began work on AutoWP, the world's first and only WordPress plugin with AI Agent and MCP support, in 2022 and released it in 2023.

We are still developing Agentic Scraper, the world's first AI Agent-powered scraper. 

MCP servers Hub, world's first marketplace for MCP servers. 

MyReplyBot, for agentic social media automations.
